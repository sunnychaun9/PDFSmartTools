# Privacy Policy

**PDF Smart Tools**
**Last updated:** March 2, 2026

## Introduction

PDF Smart Tools ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application developed for Android devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. PDF Smart Tools is designed as an offline-first application — your documents are processed entirely on your device.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Your documents**: PDF files, images, Word documents, and any other files you process with our app are NEVER uploaded to any server. All processing happens locally on your device.
- **Document content**: We do not read, analyze, store, or transmit the content of your files.
- **Personal information**: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
- **Contacts or messages**: We do not access your contacts, messages, or other personal data.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data (Anonymous)

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app:
- App opens and session duration
- Feature usage frequency (which tools are used most)
- Device type, operating system version, and screen size
- App crashes and performance metrics
- Country/region (approximate, based on device settings)

This data is anonymous and cannot be linked to any individual user.

### Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports when the app encounters an error:
- Stack trace (technical error information)
- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Memory and storage state at time of crash

Crash reports help us identify and fix bugs to improve app stability.

### Advertising Data

For users on the Free plan, we display advertisements through Google AdMob:
- AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers
- Ad interactions (impressions, clicks)
- Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how Google handles advertising data

### Purchase Information

If you subscribe to PDF Smart Tools Pro, Google Play handles all billing:
- We do not process or store payment information
- Purchase status (free/pro) is stored locally on your device
- Please refer to [Google Play's Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) for billing privacy details

## Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Camera | Document scanning feature only |
| Storage/Media | Reading PDF/image files you select for processing |
| Internet | Analytics, crash reporting, ads, and subscription verification |
| Billing | In-app subscription purchases via Google Play |

All permissions are used solely for their stated purpose. Camera access is only activated when you use the document scanning feature.

## Data Storage

- **Local only**: All files created by the app (compressed PDFs, merged documents, etc.) are saved to your device's local storage.
- **Temporary files**: The app creates temporary files during processing, which are automatically cleaned up after the operation completes.
- **Preferences**: App settings (theme, view mode) are stored locally on your device.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties, except:
- **Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics** (Google): Anonymous analytics and crash reports
- **Google AdMob**: Advertising services (Free plan users only)
- **Google Play Billing**: Subscription management

## Children's Privacy

PDF Smart Tools is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through our app, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- **Analytics data**: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months
- **Crash reports**: Retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days
- **Local app data**: Retained on your device until you clear app data or uninstall

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete local data**: Clear app data through Android Settings > Apps > PDF Smart Tools > Clear Data
- **Opt out of analytics**: Disable analytics in app Settings (when available)
- **Opt out of personalized ads**: Use Android Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Uninstall**: Removing the app deletes all local data

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- All network communication uses HTTPS encryption
- No sensitive data is stored on remote servers
- PDF encryption uses industry-standard algorithms (AES-128/256)
- The app does not have a backend server that could be compromised

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
